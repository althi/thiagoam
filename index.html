about.

Hello! I'm Thiago Almeida Martins Marques. Born in Belo Horizonte, Brazil. I’m finishing my undergaduate studies in Computer Science at Federal University of Lavras (UFLA) this year, focusing in User Experience and User Centered Design.

I've working with some companies and academic groups during my undergraduate at UFLA: programmer at DGTI / UFLA , front-end designer at Comp Júnior, member at Centro Acadêmico de Ciência da Computação, monitor at DCC / UFLA, and professor voluntary to teach informatics for eldery people at PETI CSI

I'm founder and organizer of Google Developers Group Lavras and organizer of Google Developers Group Belo Horizonte. My currently work is at Progolden Technology Solutions, as Systems Analyst and UX/UI Designer.

I love travel. For me, it is the best way to grow discovering new places and meeting new people! I had the oportunity to study English and Computer Science in a country that I love during 16 months: United States of America! I've been at Auburn University at Montgomery and Arizona State University
